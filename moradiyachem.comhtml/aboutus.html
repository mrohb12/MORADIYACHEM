The company is also contract manufacturing facility in deferent place. The Manufacturing set-up of company is around 40 km from Navi-Mumbai which is well connected by the National Highway No.17.The company has a required strength of trained techno commercial and motivated manpower in various fields of science and technology. The vigor of Company lies in the strategic and timely decision, massive infrastructure. Physical resource and a team of dedicated professional pursuing higher level of quality, productivity and excellence.

Research & Development

Moradiya Chemicals has been concentrating on Research and Development and has scaled up its investments to step-up its capabilities in research. Moradiya Chemicals R&D activities can be categorized as follows:

1. Research Intermediate development for both small and large molecules for in-house applications

• Moradiya Chemical’s Intermediate development meets the speed and quality requirements, as all the elements of research have been conducted at Mahad

• Backward integration by in-house intermediate development to efficient Marketing/ Distribution of products.

2. Process Research & Development

• In present pharmaceutical challenging environment, the process research & development chemistry requires being sharp in the industrial-scale chemistry research by not only developing the cost effective processes but also ensuring that processes are protected by Intellectual Property Rights.

• Moradiya Chemicals Process Research & Development will shape on the basic techniques of process engineering and introduce on-line analyzing and control method to yield capabilities for current process development and scale-up together with the creation of very excellent robust commercial scale routes.

• Moradiya Chemicals R&D laboratory is fully equipped for kilogram scale preparation of API intermediates.

• We have the technology for short path distillation technique by heat sensitive chemicals can be distilled efficiently. It is usually performed under high vacuum at presser less than 0.001 bar.

• Hence it provides lowest distillation temperature and short residence time. Thus reduces possibility of thermal degradation of product

3. Contract Research and Custom Synthesis and contract third party job work

• Moradiaya Chemicals Contract Research Team has excellent process scientists to play with the present challenges with synthetic process chemistry. The team has expertise in carrying out a variety of chemical reactions.

• R&D facility is equipped with fume hoods and other equipment to perform all types’ chemical reactions.

• We have a Technical team for third party job work manufacturing in plant

4. Moradiya Chemical’s Lab Scale and Plant Scale Chemistry Capabilities

• We are expertise in preparing the small molecules and scale up from milligram scale to gram scale to 500kg scale.

• Ability to make complex organic molecules at lab scale to commercial scale

• Having facility to scale-up of intermediates and final compounds.

• Multi-Stage Synthesis.
